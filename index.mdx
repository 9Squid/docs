<Steps>
  <Step title="Get your API credentials">
    Log into the Buildmarkets Partner Dashboard, navigate to **Settings → API Keys**, and generate a key pair. You'll use your `X-API-Key` and `X-API-Secret` headers on every request.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test in the sandbox">
    Point your requests at `https://dev-tapp-api.tappengine.com`. The sandbox supports every endpoint production does — with simulated KYC, order fills, and funding transfers so no real money moves.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Follow the quickstart">
    Walk through creating an investor account, submitting KYC, linking a bank, and placing your first order in under 15 minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go live">
    Switch your base URL to `https://api.tappengine.com`, complete the partner checklist, and start serving real customers.
  </Step>
</Steps>
